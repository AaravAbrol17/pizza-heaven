Our Story

Started with One
Simple Dream.

Pizza Heaven started in 2018 with one simple goal: to serve fresh, delicious pizzas made with premium ingredients at prices every family in Amritsar could afford.

What began as a small kitchen experiment turned into a beloved local landmark. Today, we're known not just for the food, but for the warmth, the laughter, and the community that gathers around our tables every day.

Our handmade dough is prepared fresh every morning. Our vegetables are sourced locally. Our cheese is imported for that perfect melt. Every pizza is made to order, never sitting under a heat lamp.

Handmade dough — crafted fresh every single morning
Premium ingredients, sourced locally where possible
No compromise — every pizza made to order
Serving Amritsar with love since 2018
